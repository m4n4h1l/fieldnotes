my role & the organization

from may to august 2025, i served as an immigration support services intern at the alliance for african assistance (aaa) in san diego. aaa is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing comprehensive services to refugees, asylees, and immigrants from diverse backgrounds, helping them achieve self-sufficiency and thrive in their new communities.
